Who's Free?

Connecting you to your friends

Find out who is free and hang out

Schedule your catch-up

WhosFree is an app to simplify the way for you to catch up with your friends.

Organising to a catch-up can be tiresome. Do you find yourself texing back and forth to see if your friends are free? Can we just find out who is free more easily? Yes, we can!

WhosFree provides you with the simplest way to connect by sharing a WhosFree calendar among a group of friends.

You can instantly see who is free and let them know you want to hang out with a simple click. You can also let others know when you are free to hang out so that they can easily connect to you.

Get Connected

Once you sign up, you can join a friend group or invite your friends to join the group. WhosFree calendar is shared among everyone in your group. You and your friends can register free time slot in the calendar.

If you want to hang out with your friends when they are free, you can simply add your interest. Your friends can do the same to your free slot.
